<h3><font color="blue">Floormap</h3>
You can find floormap on the central part of the screen.
Floormap contains ventilation and temperature information. Value of temperature cn be changed by click on the button <img src=':/Smart home/temperature.png' width="26" height="14">.
You can turn ventilator on by click on the button <img src=':/Smart home/no_ventilation.png' width="26" height="14">. Also, you  can turn ventilator off by click on the button <img src=':/Smart home/ventilation.png' width="26" height="14">.
<\p>

<h3><font color="blue">Options</h3>
Options are on the right part of the screen. There you can turn all ventilators on or off. Also, global temperature in the house can be determined.
