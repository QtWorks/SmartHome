<h3><font color="blue">Floormap</h3>
You can find floormap on the central part of the screen.
Floormap contains basic house information like temperature, lighting and alarm condition. You aren't allowed to change these parameters at this screen.
If you want to change any of these parameters, you should chose apropriate item on the navigation bar.
<\p>
