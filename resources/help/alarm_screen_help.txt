<h3><font color="blue">Floormap</h3>
You can find floormap on the central part of the screen.
Floormap contains alarm state information. Red parts represent that window or door is broken, while green show that everything is ok.
<p>
<h3><font color="blue">Options</h3>
Options are on the right part of the screen. There you can change sound alarm and turn alarm off.
